Etymology

Talinghaga

In Filipino literature, Talinghaga is the soul of the poem. It is more than a simple metaphor; it is a profound mystery wrapped in an image, a riddle that invites the reader to look deeper than the surface.

It is the art of saying everything by saying something else. Here, we embrace this tradition—providing a vessel for your most complex emotions to exist without judgment, shielded by the veil of anonymity.
